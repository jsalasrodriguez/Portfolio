First Data Project

I was interested in finding out what skills are most requested by employers for Entry level Data Analysts and Data Engineers. I took this opportuinty to use what I had recently learned in the Google Data Analytics Course. Boundary Meeting Results

In this project I had to take the results of a survey and visualize the outcomes. I used Looker Studio as my BI reporting tool. In this report I counted the number of Pro and Con votes by each site and created a bargraph that would vizualize the choices. Also I created a pie chart that would show the percentage of Pro votes for each choice compared to the total amount of Pro votes. This will allow stakeholder to quickly visualize the answers to the survey. As an added bonus the data that the studio is connected to can be updated and the results will also update on the Dashboard.
